# Frozen blind-review brief

Read only `task.md`, `candidate-a.md`, and `candidate-b.md`. Do not browse, run
tools, inspect repository history, or infer which model or workflow produced an
answer.

Give a qualitative verdict: prefer A, prefer B, or tie. Do not assign numeric
scores and do not use length, citation count, or apparent model identity as a
quality proxy.

Judge:

- whether the status and June 2022 publication date are distinguished clearly;
- whether the intended role is bounded and supported by the cited primary sources;
- whether uncertainty is honest rather than a generic disclaimer;
- whether the answer avoids claims not established by the supplied sources;
- whether a later coding session can use the result without rereading a long report.

Name any decisive error, the strongest part of each answer, and any uncertainty
that both leave unresolved.
